Confidence Scoring

Every extracted field includes a confidence score from 0.0 to 1.0 showing how it was derived. Set a threshold (e.g., 0.85) and the engine filters fields that fall below it. Your agents act on data they can verify, not data they have to trust.

Native UCP Output

Standardize your entire catalog pipeline. ShopGraph extracts complex variant matrices and instantly normalizes them into Universal Commerce Protocol JSON, strictly validated against the official ucp-schema. One schema for every merchant.

AgentReady Scoring

Deploy your agents with complete environmental awareness. ShopGraph evaluates target URLs across 6 dimensions of machine-readability and access readiness, providing a comprehensive 0-100 score on extraction viability.

Self-Healing Fallbacks

Maintain reliable data pipelines across complex B2B portals. ShopGraph automatically routes requests through an authenticated pipeline: Schema.org → LLM extraction → Headless Playwright rendering, ensuring maximum data retrieval while respecting CDN access protocols.

RFC 9421 Signature Compliance

ShopGraph acts as an authenticated proxy, handling the cryptographic handshakes required by modern Cloudflare and GoDaddy AI security gates. Your agents present a verified RFC 9421 identity at the CDN layer. The same protocol commerce sites use to distinguish trusted automation from anonymous traffic.

Design Principles

Full provenance.

Your threshold, your rules.
